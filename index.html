How it works

Click the RSS button on any show to copy its feed URL. Paste it into your favorite RSS reader (Feedly, NetNewsWire, etc.) to get notifications whenever new seasons appear.

Updates come from TVmaze data – typically announced before or around official air dates. We respect their API rate limits and check for updates regularly.
